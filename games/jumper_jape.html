Jumper Jape is a skill-based hardcore platformer

Controls

Style 1 (Keyboard)

Arrow keys Left/Right to move
Arrow key Up or Spacebar to jump
E, Z or Enter (Return) to interact

Style 2 (Keyboard)

A & D keys to move
W or Spacebar to jump
E, Z or Enter (Return) to interact
